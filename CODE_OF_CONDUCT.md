# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Hypha is a small, open project. The standards are simple:

- **Be direct, be kind.** Technical disagreement is welcome. Personal attacks are not.
- **Assume good faith.** Ask before accusing. Most misunderstandings are misunderstandings.
- **Respect scope.** Feature requests outside the project's stated scope (see README "What's explicitly deferred") will be closed with a pointer to the deferral list, not debated.
- **Credit prior art.** If your PR draws on another project's ideas, cite them.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language in any form.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing private information (doxxing), including screenshots of DMs.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may ban contributors whose behavior they consider inappropriate, threatening, or harmful — temporarily or permanently.

## Reporting

Report violations to **nalediymkekana+conduct@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
